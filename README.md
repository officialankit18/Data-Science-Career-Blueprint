## 📌 Project Overview

In today’s rapidly evolving Data Science ecosystem, success is often misunderstood as purely technical.  
This project takes a **data-driven approach** to analyze the complete professional journey—from **entry-level roles to leadership positions**.

We decode the relationship between:
- Technical skills
- Salary progression
- Behavioral and psychological traits

🎯 **Outcome:** A structured *Professional Development Blueprint* for career growth.

---

### 📁 4. Senior Data Scientist Personality (SDS)

## 💡 Key Insights & Findings

### 🔑 Technical Gatekeepers
- Python and SQL are essential for entry-level roles  

### 📈 Skill–Success Link
- Strong coding and AI/ML skills → higher salary hikes  

### 🧭 Leadership Pivot
- Leadership success depends on behavioral traits:
  - High Conscientiousness  
  - Low Neuroticism  

### 💰 Salary Convergence
- Data Science roles offer:
  - Higher salary ceilings  
  - Faster growth vs analytics roles  

### ⚠️ Market Skill Gap
- High demand for AI/ML skills  
- Low candidate proficiency  
➡️ Major upskilling opportunity
